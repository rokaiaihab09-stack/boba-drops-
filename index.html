Liza Donnelly is a writer and cartoonist known for her work with The New Yorker and her political and cultural commentary, which she has contributed to publications like CNN and CBS News. She is the author of several books, including children's books and "Cartoon Marriage: Adventures in Love and Matrimony," and is also a lecturer. Donnelly has been a key figure in the increasing visibility of female cartoonists in major publications.
